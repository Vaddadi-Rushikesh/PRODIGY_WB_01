The new called AI - Apple Intelligence

Apple Intelligence encompasses AI and machine learning technologies like Siri, Core ML, and the Neural Engine. These enable advanced features such as voice assistance, image recognition, and natural language processing while prioritizing privacy. Apple's AI enhances user experience across its devices, including iPhone, iPad, and Apple Watch.

Summarized notifications, on the other hand, provide a brief overview, allowing you to quickly scan and understand them without delving into details. Priority messages in Mail elevate urgent emails to the top of your inbox, making sure you see them first. For example, an invitation deadline for today or a reminder to check in for your afternoon flight will be highlighted. This ensures you don’t miss important updates and can respond promptly to time-sensitive matters. Apple Intelligence safeguards your privacy at every turn, deeply integrated into the heart of your iPhone, iPad, and Mac for on-device processing.
